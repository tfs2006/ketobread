Common Keto Bread Problems

  • Eggy taste: Too many eggs without proper flavor balance makes bread taste like an omelet.
  • Dense, heavy texture: Almond flour and coconut flour behave differently than wheat flour, often resulting in bricks instead of loaves.
  • Won't rise properly: Without gluten, achieving that airy, fluffy texture requires specific techniques most recipes don't explain.
